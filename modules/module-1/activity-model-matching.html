Purpose: Build your understanding of which AI model types are designed for which tasks.

Background: AI Model Types

Different AI tasks require different model architectures. Here are the main types you'll encounter:

Model Type What It Does Examples
Large Language Model (LLM) Generates and processes text; predicts next words based on patterns in training data ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini
Image Generator Creates images from text descriptions or modifies existing images DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion
Classifier Categorizes inputs into predefined categories (spam/not spam, positive/negative) Email spam filters, sentiment analysis, content moderation
Recommendation System Suggests items based on patterns in user behavior and preferences Netflix suggestions, Amazon "customers also bought," Spotify playlists
Speech Recognition Converts spoken audio to text Siri, Alexa, Google Voice typing
Computer Vision Analyzes and interprets images/video (detection, recognition, segmentation) Face recognition, self-driving car perception, medical imaging
