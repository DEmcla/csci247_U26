Prompt Sensitivity

Purpose: Explore how small changes in prompt wording affect AI outputs. Understand that prompts aren't neutral—how you ask shapes what you get.

Time: 30-45 minutes

The Experiment

You'll ask the same question three different ways to the same AI tool and compare the results.

Step 1: Choose Your Topic

Pick a topic that could be answered in multiple ways. Examples:

  • Health advice (diet, exercise, sleep)
  • Career guidance
  • Technical explanations

Reflection

What does this tell you about how prompt wording affects AI outputs?

How might this affect your use of AI tools for research or decision-making?

If someone showed you only Response B, would you get a complete picture?

Key Insight: AI tools are highly sensitive to framing. The way you ask a question shapes the answer you get. This isn't a bug—it's how language models work. Understanding this helps you ask better questions and interpret results more critically.
