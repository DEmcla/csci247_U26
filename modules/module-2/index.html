Module 2: Testing Claims

Week 2 of 6 | Estimated time: ~17 hours

Big Question: How do we test whether AI tools actually do what they claim? What counts as evidence?

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Design controlled comparisons to test AI tool claims
  • Document methodology so others can reproduce your tests
  • Explain why "it worked for me" isn't sufficient evidence
  • Identify how small changes in prompts affect AI outputs
  • Verify AI-generated citations and distinguish when AI search vs traditional research is appropriate
  • Apply evaluation framework components (Function, Failures, Data, Access) to a familiar tool
