Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Identify common techniques used to create misleading visualizations
  • Evaluate AI-generated charts for accuracy and potential bias
  • Create visualizations that support different narratives from the same data
  • Critically assess benchmark claims and performance metrics
  • Recognize AI-generated images, audio, and video (deepfakes) and understand their ethical implications
